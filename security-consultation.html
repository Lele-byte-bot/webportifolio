Security Consultation Services

Navigate the complex cybersecurity landscape with confidence. My security consultation services provide comprehensive assessments, strategic planning, and implementation guidance to help organizations build robust defenses against cyber threats while ensuring compliance with industry standards and regulations.

What I Offer

As a dedicated cybersecurity student with hands-on experience in security awareness and foundational knowledge of industry frameworks, I provide beginner-friendly security consultation that bridges the gap between complex concepts and practical implementation.

  • Beginner Security Assessments: Simple vulnerability checks and basic risk analysis to help you understand your current security posture without overwhelming technical jargon.
  • Framework-Guided Strategies: Practical security planning using frameworks like NIST and ISO 27001, explained in clear, actionable terms for organizations just starting their security journey.
  • Compliance Made Simple: Step-by-step guidance on meeting basic compliance requirements, focusing on essential controls that protect your organization without unnecessary complexity.
  • Incident Response Basics: Straightforward incident response planning that covers the fundamentals of handling security incidents, from detection to recovery.
  • Security Awareness Programs: Engaging training sessions that make cybersecurity relatable and actionable, drawing from my experience hosting Monday Mayhem sessions and community awareness initiatives.
  • Essential Security Tools: Recommendations for user-friendly security tools and basic implementations that provide immediate protection without requiring advanced technical expertise.

My Framework Knowledge

Through my cybersecurity studies and practical experience, I've developed foundational knowledge in key security frameworks:

  • NIST Cybersecurity Framework: Understanding of the core functions (Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover) and how they apply to real-world security practices.
  • ISO 27001: Knowledge of information security management systems and the importance of systematic risk management approaches.
  • Basic Risk Assessment: Skills in identifying common security risks and prioritizing them based on likelihood and impact.
  • GDPR Fundamentals: Understanding of data protection principles and basic compliance requirements for handling personal information.

Key Focus Areas for Beginners

  • Practical Risk Management: Simple approaches to identifying and addressing the most common security risks that affect small businesses and growing organizations.
  • Everyday Threat Awareness: Education on real-world threats like phishing, password security, and safe internet practices that everyone can understand and implement.
  • Basic Access Control: Straightforward guidance on protecting sensitive information through simple authentication and authorization practices.
  • Data Protection Basics: Essential strategies for keeping customer and business data safe, explained in non-technical terms.
  • Network Security Fundamentals: Basic concepts for securing networks and devices, focusing on practical steps anyone can take.
  • Cloud Security Starting Points: Beginner-friendly guidance for securing cloud services and understanding shared responsibility models.

Simple Consultation Process

  1. Friendly Chat: A relaxed conversation to understand your current security situation and what keeps you up at night.
  2. Easy Assessment: Simple evaluation of your basic security measures using straightforward checklists and common-sense questions.
  3. Clear Recommendations: Practical, prioritized suggestions that you can actually implement, explained in plain language.
