Tech Advocacy

As a Cybersecurity Awareness Activist and passionate advocate for digital literacy, I champion inclusive tech spaces where girls, youth, and underserved communities can thrive. My advocacy work is rooted in lived experience — from paneling at Girls in ICT Day to leading campus initiatives that spotlight cybersecurity and AI.

What I Offer

  • Community Outreach & Campaigns: Designing and executing awareness campaigns that demystify cybersecurity, AI, and digital safety for everyday users.
  • Youth & Women Empowerment: Hosting workshops, webinars, and hackathons that center girls' perspectives in tech design and innovation.
  • Strategic Storytelling: Crafting emotionally resonant narratives that inspire action, policy change, and cultural shifts in tech spaces.
  • Event Participation & Representation: Speaking, moderating, and facilitating at events that promote digital inclusion, gender equity, and tech for good.

Impact Highlights

  • Panelist at Girls in ICT Day 2025, sharing insights on how girls' perspectives shape secure, inclusive digital solutions.
  • Featured in UN Women's Women's Month Commemoration, advocating for gender parity and coalition-building in tech.
  • Recognized by Elevate Trust and OmniContact International for leadership in coding, cybersecurity, and academic affairs.
